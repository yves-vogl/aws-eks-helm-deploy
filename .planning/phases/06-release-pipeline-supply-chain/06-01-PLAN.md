<objective>
Refactor `.github/workflows/ci.yml` from the current 1-job pre-commit runner into the 7-job parallel fan-out specified by CONTEXT D9. Each job is independent and runs in parallel; failure surfaces the specific bad job for fast triage. This plan also ships the two structural pytest files (`test_ci_yml_structure.py` and `test_workflow_digest_pins.py`) that downstream plans depend on — every subsequent Phase 6 workflow file added in waves 2–5 must pass these structural gates.

Purpose: CI-01 (gate every PR with lint/typecheck/unit/integration/trivy/pip-audit/acceptance) + SEC-04 (Trivy on image, Dockerfile, chart fixtures, secret-leak) + SEC-05 (pip-audit on every PR) are foundation REQs — the rest of Phase 6 (release pipeline, sign-and-attest, supply-chain rescan, governance) all assume this gate is in place. Pitfall #1 ("id-token: write in pull_request jobs") is enforced structurally by the `test_workflow_digest_pins.py` companion test and by NOT requesting any id-token permission anywhere in ci.yml.

Output:
- A rewritten `.github/workflows/ci.yml` with workflow-level `permissions: contents: read`, the `concurrency` block, and the 7 fan-out jobs (each pinned to its required action SHAs from 06-RESEARCH.md "Action digest resolution").
- A new `tests/structural/` test root with `__init__.py` and two structural test files used by every downstream Phase 6 plan.
